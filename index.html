Story

The Boy with the Nine-Tails

Long ago, the Nine-Tailed Fox (Kurama) attacked the Hidden Leaf Village. The Fourth Hokage, Minato Namikaze, sealed the beast inside his newborn son, Naruto Uzumaki, giving his life in the process. Orphaned and shunned by the villagers who feared the demon within him, Naruto grew up lonely but dreamed of becoming Hokage so everyone would acknowledge him.

Becoming a Ninja

After barely graduating from the Ninja Academy, Naruto was placed in Team 7 with Sasuke Uchiha and Sakura Haruno under their leader, Kakashi Hatake. Through dangerous missions, Naruto began proving himself. He formed rivalries and friendships, most notably with Sasuke, whose tragic past drove him to seek revenge.

Chasing Sasuke

Sasuke eventually left the village to gain power from Orochimaru, a rogue ninja. Determined to bring his friend back, Naruto trained under Jiraiya, one of the Legendary Sannin, to grow stronger.

The Kazekage Rescue

Two and a half years later, Naruto returns stronger. Akatsuki, a group of rogue ninja, kidnaps Gaara, now Kazekage. Naruto and his team rescue him, deepening their bonds.
